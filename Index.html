Our Core Beliefs

We are a non-denominational Christian church committed to the infallible word of God. Our faith is rooted in the foundational truths of the Gospel.

The Holy Bible

We believe the Holy Bible is the inspired Word of God, the supreme and final authority in all matters of faith and conduct. It is the complete revelation of God's will for humanity.

The Trinity

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit. They are co-equal and co-eternal, yet distinct in their roles.

Salvation through Christ

We believe that salvation is a gift from God, offered through grace alone, received through faith alone, in Christ alone. We are justified by His atoning death and resurrected life.

Serving Our Community in Banda, Kampala

Our faith is put into action through active outreach in our local community. We believe in being the hands and feet of Christ, serving our neighbors with love and compassion.

Youth Empowerment

We provide mentorship and vocational training programs to empower the youth of Banda, equipping them with skills for a brighter future.

Food for the Hungry

Our volunteers regularly organize food distribution drives to support families in need within the Banda community.

Health and Wellness

We host free medical camps to provide basic healthcare services and wellness education to those who lack access to medical facilities.
